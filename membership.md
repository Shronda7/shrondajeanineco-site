## FAQ

**Can I change tiers?**  
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Is there a commitment?**  
No long-term contracts. Cancel anytime. We believe in earned retention, not locked-in subscriptions.

**What if I can't afford it?**  
Contact us. We reserve a limited number of sliding-scale spots for those in need.

**How do I access live sessions?**  
All live sessions are hosted via secure video link. Recordings are available within 24 hours for members who cannot attend live.
