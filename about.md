## The Origin

Shronda Jeanine & Company was born from a single conviction: **healing is not a luxury. It is a birthright.**

After years of witnessing the fragmentation that stress, trauma, and systemic neglect create in individuals and communities, I committed to building something different. A space where wholeness is not marketed as a product but practiced as a principle. Where abundance is not measured in accumulation but in alignment.

## The Philosophy

We operate from three core beliefs:

### 1. Wholeness Over Perfection

You do not need to be "fixed." You need to be gathered. Every part of your experience — the polished and the painful — belongs in the whole.

### 2. Abundance From Alignment

External success without internal peace is not abundance. It is performance. True abundance flows from a life aligned with your values, your body, and your purpose.

### 3. Community as Catalyst

Healing accelerates in connection. The lone journey is slower, harder, and less sustainable. We are built for relationship — with ourselves, with others, and with something larger.

## The Work

Based in **Phoenix, Arizona**, we offer:
- Individual sessions (virtual and phone)
- Group healing circles
- Membership community with resources and live events
- Crisis support and referral navigation

Every creation carries the signature of this work — visible in care, concealed in craft.
